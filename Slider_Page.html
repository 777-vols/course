Traveler in Belarus

These are just a few reasons why you should go to Belarus.

Don't need a passport

No visas, insurance, hotel confirmations or other paperwork. At the border, they will only ask for a passport and check the suitcase. In 2021, for recreation in Belarus, you do not need to take a COVID-19 test - a good economy of budget and psychological health savings.

Very tasty food!

Belarus is not only potatoes. Although the potato pancakes are as tasty as your grandmother's. Try Belarusian sweets, a breathtaking chicken roll at a butcher's shop, buy homemade tomatoes at the market, go to a supermarket for natural dairy products. The list can be endless ...

A lot of entertainment

Casino lovers like to visit Minsk. For cultural recreation - theaters, exhibitions, educational excursions, museums. Do you love nature? Go to Belovezhskaya Pushcha.

Ecotourism in Belarus

Thanks to the unique nature with ancient forests and swamps, clean lakes and rivers, as well as an amazing variety of flora and fauna, Belarus is a special country for lovers of ecological tourism.

Clean and safe

Compared to many European cities, the capital of Belarus is clean and tidy even in the metro. No papers, bottles or other dirt. This is impressive. It's also safe here. You can safely walk around the city at night.
